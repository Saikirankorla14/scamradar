About ScamRadar

An AI-powered scam awareness training platform for education, research, and fraud prevention.

⚠ Educational use only. ScamRadar generates fictional scam mockups solely to help people recognize real fraud. All generated content is clearly labeled as educational material and must never be used to deceive or defraud real people. Misuse is illegal and unethical.

Americans lost $10B+ to fraud in 2023 according to the FTC. The most effective defense against scams is awareness — people who understand how scams work are significantly less likely to fall victim to them.

ScamRadar uses AI to generate highly realistic scam conversation mockups across multiple formats (phone calls, SMS, emails, voicemails, and more), helping individuals, educators, and security researchers study the exact tactics scammers use without requiring exposure to real criminal content.

Features
✓Generator — Create realistic scam mockups for 14 scam types across 6 delivery formats with adjustable pressure and target profiles
✓Analyzer — Paste any suspicious message and get AI-powered analysis of red flags, confidence scores, and scam type identification
✓Red flag detection — Every generated mockup comes with detailed explanations of the specific manipulation tactics used
✓Library — Save and organize generated mockups for training sessions and reference
✓History — Session history keeps your recent generations easily accessible
